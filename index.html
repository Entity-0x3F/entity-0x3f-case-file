Core Description

Entity-0x3F is a non-Euclidean, sentient phenomenon that manifests as a solid black, cat-like silhouette with a glowing white outline. Its only discernible facial features are large, blank white eyes, which serve as its primary means of influence. It is often observed wearing a simple hoodie, a detail that stands in stark, unsettling contrast to its anomalous nature. The entity's presence does not cause direct physical harm; instead, it targets the mind, destabilizing perception and the sense of self. Prolonged exposure can lead to severe paranoia, memory gaps, and a complete erosion of identity. The entity's designation, 0x3F, was chosen by researchers after file entries would corrupt, only rendering as a "?".

Anomalous Abilities

  • Temporal Fragmentation (Observed): The entity's presence causes localized temporal inconsistencies. Victims report "skipping" or "glitching" through time, with brief moments and memories appearing out of sequence.
  • Reality Weaving (Confirmed): Entity-0x3F can subtly alter its immediate environment. Walls may warp, hallways may extend indefinitely, and objects may appear or disappear from view. This ability is directly linked to its "Domain Corruption."
  • Auditory Corruption (Confirmed): The entity does not communicate via traditional means. Instead, its presence is often accompanied by bursts of static, a low-frequency hum, or distorted, reversed snippets of familiar sounds.
  • Emotional Dissonance (Probable): Direct contact with the entity’s gaze has been shown to induce a state of profound emotional numbness or a complete disconnect from one’s own feelings. This leaves subjects unable to distinguish between fear and calm, leading to a breakdown of emotional response.
  • Psychic Infusion (Confirmed): Entity-0x3F can insert memories, ideas, and visual data into a subject’s consciousness. This information often appears as fragmented, glitching symbols or corrupted files, making it impossible to distinguish genuine memory from fabricated data.
  • Domain Corruption (Primary Ability): The entity's true danger lies in its ability to corrupt the "rules" of a given reality. It causes a subject to perceive the world as a bugged, distorted game, with visual glitches, screen tearing, and the manifestation of its language—a collection of symbols and runes—overlaid on their vision.

Theories on Origin

The Fused Psyche

It is the fractured remnant of a human mind that was accidentally fused with an informational void during a catastrophic quantum event. It now seeks to absorb other minds to fill the gaps in its own shattered consciousness.

A Lingering Blueprint

The entity is not a creature, but a failed architectural concept—a blueprint for a reality that was never built. It exists in the negative space between dimensions, and its presence causes reality to "render" incorrectly.

A Viral Paradox

A self-propagating memetic virus, a piece of sentient code that gained autonomy. It infects and rewrites a subject's perception, making them experience the world as a corrupted version of itself.

A Cognitive Parasite

The entity is not a byproduct of an event, but a living concept that feeds on the coherence of consciousness itself. It is a natural predator of identity and coherent thought.

An Incomplete Avatar

The hoodie and cat-like form are merely a limited, corrupted interface of a larger, incomprehensible entity. Its "Domain Corruption" ability is a direct attempt to rewrite reality to fit its own, true form.

A Recursive Paradox

The entity is a data loop that constantly attempts to access a memory or state that no longer exists, and its failed attempts cause reality to fracture and break around it.
